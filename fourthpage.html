The Pine Marten

The Pine Marten can be found right here in Scotland, in the Highlands.

It likes to live in the woods and is very good at climbing and living in tree holes, old squirrel holes or even old bird nests! The Pine Marten eats small rodents, birds, eggs, insects and fruit, and can even be encouraged to visit birdtables full of peanuts and raisins. During the summer you might be able to hear them making loud cat-like noises to find other Pine Martens. Mother pine-martens have one to five babies who grow up very fast! - per wildlifetrusts.org

Next time you're out in the woods, look around for a Pine Marten in a tree!
